Party Decoration Essentials

  • Balloons & helium
  • Curling ribbon for tying and hanging balloons
  • Scissors (general use)
  • Command hooks for attaching bunting and garlands
  • Sticky-tac or sticky-tape (just in case)
  • Table confetti for decoration

Party Food & Drink Essentials

  • Plenty of napkins & hand-wipes for sticky fingers
  • Party food boxes / party plates / cutlery
  • Serving platters / cupcake stands for displaying buffet food
  • Jugs, cups & straws (have extras for helpers)
  • Cocktail sticks or food skewers for kebabs
